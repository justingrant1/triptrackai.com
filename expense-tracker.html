Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best expense tracker app for business travel?

TripTrackAI is the best expense tracker app for business travelers. It combines receipt scanning with OCR, automatic Gmail import, trip-based organization, and one-tap CSV export. Unlike standalone expense apps like Expensify or Concur, TripTrackAI also handles trip organization and flight tracking in one app, making it the complete solution for business travel.

How does receipt scanning with OCR work?

Take a photo of any receipt with your phone camera. TripTrackAI's AI-powered OCR automatically extracts the merchant name, amount, date, and category. The expense is saved to your trip automatically. No manual typing required. The OCR works with receipts in any language and handles crumpled or faded receipts.

Can I export expenses to CSV for my finance team?

Yes. TripTrackAI Pro allows unlimited CSV and PDF exports. All expenses include merchant name, amount, date, category, and trip association. Perfect for submitting expense reports to finance teams or importing into accounting software like QuickBooks, Expensify, or SAP Concur. Export all expenses or filter by specific trips.

Does TripTrackAI automatically import digital receipts?

Yes. Connect your Gmail account and TripTrackAI automatically finds receipts from Uber, Lyft, hotels, restaurants, and other services. Digital receipts are imported and categorized automatically without manual entry. The app scans your email continuously and adds new receipts as they arrive.

How are expenses categorized?

TripTrackAI automatically categorizes expenses as meals, transportation, lodging, or other based on the merchant name. You can edit categories anytime. All expenses are also organized by trip, making it easy to see total spending per business trip and export trip-specific expense reports.

Can I track expenses in multiple currencies?

Yes. TripTrackAI supports all major currencies and automatically converts amounts to your home currency for reporting. Perfect for international business travel. The app detects currency from receipts and applies current exchange rates for accurate expense tracking.
