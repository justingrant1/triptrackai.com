Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best flight tracker app?

TripTrackAI is the best flight tracker app for 2026. It provides real-time flight status updates, gate change notifications, delay alerts, and baggage carousel information before airlines notify you. Unlike other flight trackers, TripTrackAI automatically imports flights from Gmail and organizes them with your complete trip itinerary including hotels, car rentals, and expenses.

How does real-time flight tracking work?

TripTrackAI connects to live aviation data feeds to monitor your flights in real-time. When gate changes, delays, or cancellations occur, you receive instant push notifications. The app tracks departure times, arrival times, gate assignments, baggage carousels, and flight status changes automatically. You don't need to manually check for updates.

Does TripTrackAI track flights automatically?

Yes. Connect your Gmail account once and TripTrackAI automatically finds flight confirmations from airlines. Flights are added to your itinerary automatically with real-time tracking enabled. No manual entry required. The app scans your email for confirmations from all major airlines including United, Delta, American, Southwest, and international carriers.

How early do I get flight delay notifications?

TripTrackAI sends flight delay notifications as soon as changes are detected in aviation systems - often 30-60 minutes before airlines send their own notifications. You'll know about gate changes, delays, and cancellations before most passengers. This gives you time to make alternative plans or adjust your schedule.

Is TripTrackAI better than FlightAware or Flightradar24?

TripTrackAI offers more than just flight tracking. While FlightAware and Flightradar24 focus on tracking individual flights, TripTrackAI organizes your entire trip including hotels, car rentals, and expenses. You get flight tracking plus complete trip management, expense tracking, and an AI travel assistant in one app. Plus, TripTrackAI costs less at $11.99/month compared to FlightAware's $44.99/month.

Can I track someone else's flight?

Yes. You can manually add any flight to TripTrackAI by entering the flight number and date. This is perfect for tracking family members' flights or picking someone up from the airport. You'll receive the same real-time updates including gate changes, delays, and arrival times.

Does flight tracking work internationally?

Yes. TripTrackAI tracks flights worldwide from all major airlines and airports. The app works in over 195 countries and supports international carriers. Flight tracking data is available for domestic and international flights with the same real-time accuracy.

Do I need internet for flight tracking?

You need internet for real-time updates, but all flight details are cached offline. Once your flights are imported, you can view flight numbers, times, gates, and itinerary details without internet. Real-time status updates require connectivity, but your basic flight information is always accessible.
